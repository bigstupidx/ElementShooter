How-to-play:
1. Enemies will come from right side of the map.
2. Shoot enemies with superior element compare to its element to destroy them.
3. Higher level will have different enemies with different ability to pop up.

Element Chart:
- Fire > Earth        - Dark > Light
- Earth > Thunder     - Light > Dark
- Thunder > Water
- Water > Fire
